Find My Representative

Project brief

For this project, I utilized the Google Civics API to allow a user to enter their residential address and show them all of their government representatives from federal to local level and contact them by mail, phone, and social media. I integrated Google Places API to auto-complete addresses and developed the back end with Node.js and Express. During election season, this app also will tell you your polling location and the hours of operation.
